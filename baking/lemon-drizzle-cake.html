Lemon Drizzle Cake

Ingredients

The "Flour Mixture"

  • 300g Flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 3/4 tsp Salt

The "Base Mixture"

  • 250g Unsalted Butter
  • 300g Granulated Sugar
  • Zest of 2 Lemons
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tbsp Vanilla Extract

The "Lemon Mixture"

  • 200g Plain Yoghurt
  • 40ml Milk
  • Juice of 2 Lemons

Method

  • Create the Flour Mixture and the Lemon mixture in two separate bowls and mix well.
  • Create the Base Mixture by creaming butter in a mixer, then add sugar and the zest of the two lemons. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  • Add the eggs one at a time until fluffy.
  • While the mixer is on, add the Flour Mixture and the Lemon Mixture, a third at a time, alternating between the two until well combined.
  • Bake at 170C for 35-40 minutes until the skewer comes out clean.
