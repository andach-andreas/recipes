Cheese Scones

Ingredients

  • 225g Self-Raising Flour or 225g Plain Flour with 3tsp Baking Powder
  • Pinch of Salt
  • 1 extra tsp Baking Powder
  • 55g Chilled Butter, cut into cubes
  • 120g Chedder or other Cheese
  • 90ml Milk

Method

  • Sieve flour into a bowl with the salt and baking powder.
  • Mix butter in with fingertips until it turns into breadcrumbs.
  • Mix in 90g of the cheese.
  • Mix in the milk to form a soft dough. Avoid using your hands here, use a pallet knife as much as possible.
  • Turn out onto a floured surface and knead briefly. Very briefly, you're basically just making it come together. 5 'kneads' is plenty.
  • Roll out to 2cm thick and cut into rounds with a pastry cutter.
  • Place on a baking tray and brush with milk. Top with the remaining cheese.
  • Bake at 200C/180C fan for 12-15 minutes.
