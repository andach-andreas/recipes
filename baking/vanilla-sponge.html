Vanilla Sponge

Ingredients

  • 200g Caster Sugar
  • 200g Self-Raising Flour
  • 200g Softened Butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1tsp Baking Powder
  • 2tbsp Milk
  • 1tbsp Vanilla Extract

for the filling

  • 100g Softened Butter
  • 140g Icing Sugar, sifted
  • 150g Jam

Method

Sponge

  • Combine all ingredients in a mixer and mix well.
  • Divide into two 20cm circular tins. Smooth the top with a pallette knife.
  • Bake at 190C (170C fan) for 20 minutes.

Filling

  • Cream the butter until smooth in a mixer.
  • Add the icing sugar slowly until you have a smooth paste.

Construction

  • Allow the cakes to cool for 10 mins, then remove from the tins. Allow to cool fully on a cooling rack.
  • Trim the bottom cake to even out the dome.
  • Spread the filling mixture onto the bottom cake, then top with jam, generously.
  • Sprinkle with icing sugar to serve. Store in an airtight container.
