Satay Easy Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) Coconut Oil
  • 1 tbsp Red Curry Paste
  • 1 tbsp Vegetable Oil or Sesame Oil
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 6 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • Small Handful Finely Chopped Peanuts (optional, for texture)

Method

  • Fry the Red Curry Paste in the hot oil for 2 minutes, to release the flavours.
  • Add the Coconut Oil, bring to a very light boil.
  • Add the sugar, salt and half the peanut butter. Add the rest of the peanut butter slowly and stir continuously for 3-5 minutes until the sauce achieves desired texture.
  • Add the finely chopped peanuts at the end for texture.
