Brandy/Cognac Cocktails

Alexander

  • 1 Cognac
  • 1 Creme de Cacao
  • 1 Fresh Cream

Serve short in a cocktail glass. Garnish with ground nutmeg.

Angel Face (also in Gin)

  • 1 Gin
  • 1 Apricot Brandy
  • 1 Calvados (Apple/Pear Brandy)

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass.

Between The Sheets (also in Rum)

  • 1 White Rum
  • 1 Cognac
  • 1 Triple Sec
  • 2/3 Lemon Juice

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass.

Brandy Crusta

  • 3 Brandy
  • 1 Lemon Juice
  • 1/2 Cherry Liquer
  • Dash Orange Liquer
  • Dash Sugar Syrup
  • Dash Bitters

Serve short in a cocktail glass. Garnish with orange or lemon peel slice.

Paradise (also in Gin)

  • 3 Gin
  • 2 Apricot Brandy
  • 2 Orange Juice

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass.

Porto Flip

  • 1 Brandy
  • 3 Port
  • Some Egg Yolk

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with ground nutmeg.

Sazerac

  • 5 Cognac
  • 1 Absinthe
  • 1 Sugar Syrup
  • Dash Bitters

Serve in an old fashioned glass. Garnish with lemon peel.

Sidecar

  • 2 Cognac
  • 1 Triple Sec
  • 1 Lemon Juice

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass.

Stinger

  • 2 Cognac
  • 1 White Creme de Menthe

Serve in a chilled martini glass.

Vieux Carré (also in Whiskey)

  • 1 Whiskey
  • 1 Cognac
  • 1 Sweet Vermouth
  • Dash Bitters
  • Dash Bénédictine

Serve in a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a cherry and orange zest.
