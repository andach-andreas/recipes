Whiskey Cocktails

Boulevardier

  • 3 Whiskey / Bourbon
  • 2 Bitter Campari
  • 2 Sweet Red Vermouth

Serve in a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with orange peel.

Manhattan

  • 2 Whiskey / Bourbon
  • 1 Vermouth
  • Splash Cherry Liquer
  • Dash Bitters

Garnish with Cherry and a Lemon Twist.

Old Fashioned

  • 2 Whiskey / Bourbon
  • 1/2 Sugar Syrup or 1 tsp Sugar
  • Dash Bitters
  • Splash Soda Water (optional)

Garnish with an orange slice and cherry.

Rusty Nail

  • 3 Scotch Whiskey
  • 2 Drambuie (Honey Liquer)

Serve over ice in an old fashioned glass. Garnish with lemon zest.

Vieux Carré (also in Brandy)

  • 1 Whiskey
  • 1 Cognac
  • 1 Sweet Vermouth
  • Dash Bitters
  • Dash Bénédictine

Serve in a chilled martini glass. Garnish with a cherry and orange zest.
