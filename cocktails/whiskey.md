# Whiskey Cocktails
## Manhattan
* 2 Whiskey / Bourbon
* 1 Vermouth
* Splash Cherry Liquer
* Dash Bitters

Garnish with Cherry and a Lemon Twist

## Old Fashioned
* 2 Whiskey / Bourbon
* 1/2 Sugar Syrup or 1 tsp Sugar
* Dash Bitters
* Splash Soda Water (optional)

Garnish with an orange slice and cherry.
