Tequila Cocktails

Ginger Tequila

  • 2 Tequila
  • 4 Ginger Beer
  • 1/2 Sugar Surup
  • 1/2 Grenadine

Stirred, not shaken.

Margarita

  • 2 Tequila
  • 1 Lime Juice
  • 1 Orange Liquer

Serve on a salted glass. Garnish with a lime wedge.

Tequila Sunrise

  • 2 Tequila
  • Juice of 1/2 Lemon
  • 1 Orange Liquer

Serve long with orange juice. Add a splash of grenadine to each glass to create "sunrise". Garnish with a cherry.
