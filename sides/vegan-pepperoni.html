Vegan Pepperoni

Ingredients

  • 1 Beetroot
  • 4 tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 4 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 4 tbsp Vegetable Stock
  • 4 tbsp Nutritional Yeast
  • 1 tbsp Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Fennel Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Garlic Powder
  • 1 tbsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tbsp Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbsp Chili Flakes, or to taste
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Liquid Smoke
  • 1-2 cups Vital Wheat Gluten

Method

  • Combine all ingredients except vital wheat gluten in a blender and blend until smooth.
  • Take mixture out of blender and add the vital wheat gluten until combined and the mixture makes a ball.
  • Form pepperoni shapes roughly one inch in diameter, length as required by your steaming equipment.
  • Steam over a high heat for 30 minutes.
