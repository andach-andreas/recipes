Cream and Garlic Cabbage

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Onion, finely chopped
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, finely chopped
  • 1/2 a Savoy or White Cabbage, finely chopped
  • Thumb-Sized Piece of Ginger, grated
  • Generous knob of butter or spread
  • 150ml Cream or Oat Cream

Method

  • Fry the onion in the butter for 10 minutes, until translucent.
  • Add the garlic and ginger and continue to fry for another 5-10 minutes on a low heat.
  • Add the cabbage and fry for 15 minutes.
  • Add the cream and heat through for 5 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste.
